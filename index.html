I am Rudolf Lowe Bihag, a licensed Chief Marine Engineer who recently made a career shift. Throughout my years of service in the World's International Merchant Marine Fleet as a Marine Engineer Officer, I gained valuable experience and had the opportunity to explore different cultures and meet people from diverse backgrounds. While traversing the globe, I witnessed the beauty of nature in all its splendor, while also enduring its harsh and unforgiving aspects. However, as time went on, I came to the realization that a career at sea was not aligned with my personal goals. Spending months, or even an entire year, away from my loved ones and isolated from society, particularly in the vast expanses of the ocean, no longer felt worthwhile. Fortunately, my discovery of Zuitt Coding Bootcamp as a career shifter has illuminated my path toward the tech industry. I eagerly anticipate embarking on a career in this field as it aligns with my inherent passion for all things tech-related. Moreover, the idea of no longer having to be separated from my family fills me with excitement and motivation for the future.
